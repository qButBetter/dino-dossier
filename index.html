Spinosaurus

(Spinosaurus aegyptiacus)

Description

The Spinosaurus is a dinosaur from the Late Cretaceous Period. It lived around 99 million to 93.5 million years ago. There are very many relatives of the Spinosaurus, such as the Irritator and the Baryonx.
